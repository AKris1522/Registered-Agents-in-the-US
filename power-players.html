Introduction

A small group of highly influential Registered Agent companies are driving modernization, legislation, and compliance innovation across the United States. Their influence is reshaping how businesses interact with state governments.

Top Five Power Players

1. CT Corporation (Wolters Kluwer)

  • Market leader in legal services integration.
  • Leads Model Law initiatives and promotes Universal RA adoption.

2. CSC Global

  • Pioneer of backend integration between RAs and Secretary of State portals.
  • Major advocate for mandatory e-service of process legislation.

3. Northwest Registered Agent

  • Privacy-first advocate for business owners.
  • Influences legislation to protect owner anonymity and data privacy.

4. Harbor Compliance

  • Technology-driven compliance platform.
  • Focuses on automating business licensing and filings.

5. Cogency Global (formerly NRAI)

  • Specializes in international corporate compliance harmonization.
  • Advocates for multinational Registered Agent standards alignment.
