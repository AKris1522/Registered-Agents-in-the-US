Introduction

Five major companies dominate the Registered Agent industry, influencing not just filings but broader corporate governance, compliance technology, and Secretary of State relationships nationwide.

Top Five Registered Agent Companies

1. CT Corporation (Wolters Kluwer)

  • Founded: 1892
  • Serves over 1.6 million entities
  • Key focus: Legal ecosystem integration, model law leadership

2. CSC Global

  • Founded: 1899
  • Known for e-filing system partnerships with states
  • Key focus: Portal modernization, mandatory e-service advocacy

3. Cogency Global (formerly NRAI)

  • Focus: Global compliance support and harmonization
  • Influence: Quiet internationalization of registered agent standards

4. InCorp Services

  • Focus: Small to mid-sized business compliance technology
  • Strength: SaaS and automation in entity management

5. Northwest Registered Agent

  • Focus: Business privacy and owner protection
  • Key influence: Advocating for address masking and privacy laws
